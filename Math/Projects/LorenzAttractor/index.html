Lorenz Attractor

Equations:

  • Δx = σ(y - x) * Δt
  • Δy = (x(ρ - z) - y) * Δt
  • Δz = (xy - βz) * Δt
  • Where x,y,z are coordinates on the 3D Cartesian Coordinate Plane
  • Δ = Greek Letter Delta. Δx,y,z,time symbolizes differences in x,y,z,time
  • σ = Greek Letter Sigma. σ represents the Prandtl Number.
  • ρ = Greek Letter Rho. ρ represents the Rayleigh Number.
  • β = Greek Letter Beta. &beha; represents the Geometric Factor.
